What You Get

AI-Powered Sysadmin

Claude Code handles system administration tasks with human oversight. Monitoring, automation, and maintenance at your fingertips.

Multi-Device Coordination

Connect your machines via Tailscale mesh networking. Spawn tasks on remote servers, run local LLMs, coordinate across your infrastructure.

Multiple Interfaces

Access your agent via terminal, Telegram bot, or web chat. Choose what works for your workflow.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your hardware. No third-party storage. Direct API calls to Anthropic with nothing in between.

Human-Curated, AI-Enabled

ThinxAI follows the HCAE model: you make decisions, AI executes. All automation requires explicit approval. Transparency over magic.
